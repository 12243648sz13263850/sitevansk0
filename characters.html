Some of the Characters in the Game

New Character Update Everyday

Cat Default

He is the default, everyone starts with him, he is cute but fierce in the in game store you can buy clothes to dress him up.

Dark Cat

This Cat is rare, you can get it in the store or in the chests during the game with great luck, it has several powers involving darkness, and being one of the strongest cats in the entire game.

Sneaky Cat

This Cat when a battle is detected it hides both in the leaves because of its leafy part on its head and also in rocks because of its paws, it waits for the right moment to attack the enemy while it is hidden.

Wise Cat

this type of cat is super rare, it is not known exactly how old he is, but he is from the ancient times of the old cats, he is the oldest of the cats still alive, his age can reach up to 300 years old, with very extensive knowledge and unusual powers.

Halloween Version

Every year a halloween skin is given for free to all players who enter on halloween day, this edition comes free in 2022 for all players having halloween related skills.
